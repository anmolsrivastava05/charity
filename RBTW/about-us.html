Welcome to Raj Bhanwar Welfare Trust

Clean & Elegant Joomla Charity Template

The trust has a long history. It has been working since last 12 years under the guidance and leadership of Sh. Bhanwar Singh, motivated by his religious and philanthropic attitude. In personal capacity, lots of charitable and welfare activities were carried out for the rural development, animal care, and social issues etc in the villages of Jodhpur district by him. The activities mainly involved were financial assistance and ration supply to the widow women/needy and poor people; animal protection, support to farmers and weaker section of the village. The support was provided in kind or cash, as per the requirement. People themselves started approaching to seek help. Gradually, this humanitarian contribution became a continuous feature. It started working in Kota also. Hence, it was thought to thrive as a formal set-up and the idea of Raj Bhanwar Welfare Trust was emerged.In the year 2012, the trust got its formal structure with the registration in Kota andit started with systematic programmatic and organizational governance.

Established in 2012,RAJ BHANWAR WELFARE TRUST is a non-profit voluntary organisation. It works for the welfare and empowerment of underprivileged women, children, elderly and physically challenged individuals by enriching their lives and giving them access to education, health and livelihood skills.RAJ BHANWAR is a public-private partnership enabler working with several corporates, other NPO’s & NGO’s and individuals in areas of health, education & livelihood for vulnerable and at-risk children, families and communities.
