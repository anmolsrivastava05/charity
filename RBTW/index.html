About us

RAJ BHANWAR WELFARE TRUST , is a registered charity that was established over 24 years ago by Mr M S Chouhan. It was set up in order to tackle the problems of poverty in all over world, especially amongst young children. From its humble origins as a family institution it has grown rapidly, and now its projects cover a wide range of activities located in both India and other developing countries, including Afghanistan, Sudan, Kenya and Burundi.   Rah Bharwan WELFARE TRUST is committed to the welfare of people from the most underprivileged strata of society without distinction of caste, creed or color. Its emphasis is on assisting the children and youth who are the citizens of tomorrow and the disabled who need a support to help them join mainstream society.   . Our logo symbolises our vision for the future – a future in which each and every child can look forward to a new day, a day free of hunger, suffering, abuse and poverty. We want to reach out to children across the world, to give them new opportunities, and help them rise out of poverty.

  • Who we are

    Established in 2012,RAJ BHANWAR WELFARE TRUST is a non-profit voluntary organisation. It works for the welfare and empowerment of underprivileged women, children, elderly and physically challenged individuals by enriching their lives and giving them access to education, health and livelihood skills.RAJ BHANWAR is a public-private partnership enabler working with several corporates, other NPO’s & NGO’s and individuals in areas of health, education & livelihood for vulnerable and at-risk children, families and communities.
