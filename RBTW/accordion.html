  • Financial Year: 2012-13

    Total Expenses: Rs.7, 75,173.71

    1. 1.Sponsored the Mega blood donation and free health check-up camp at Kota, organised by Sai Jan KalyanSewaSamiti and Ashapoorna Ayurveda Health Care.The camp benefitted to approx 70 people.
    2. 2.Donation of a washing machine to a shelter home run by MadhuSmritiSansthan, Kota. The home shelters 170 children.
    3. 3.Food arrangement assistance to the shelter home run by MadhuSmritiSansthan, Kota.
    4. 4.Eye operation assistance to the needy people, free of charge.40 people benefitted from the camp.
    5. 5.Construction of college entrance gate of BZS Educational Society, Jodhpur, which enhanced the beauty of the college premises. Besides, provided protection from anti- social elements and stray animals.
    6. 6.Monthly distribution of ration/clothes and other items to the poor and needy families.

  • Current Financial Year: 2016-17

    Total Expenses: Rs. 10, 76,329.36

    1. 1. For the spiritual awakening, the trust got printed and distributed the Shri Ram Naam-Dhan (Sangrah) Bank’ booklets and copies of ‘Shri Krishna Leela’ and ‘ShrimadBhagwatGeeta’ in Jodhpur and Kota. These are available free of cost from trust’s office. On the occasion of launching of RamNaam Booklets at Ganesh Mandir campus, Kota, the trust also made a donation of Rs.5100/- for the construction work at Mandir.


    2. 2. Feast to Sadhus at Nasik - Kumbh Maharashtra.
    3. 3. Felicitation of the winners of free style wrestling-under 19 at National School’s Sports Competitions. ShubhamSen, bronze medal winner and AdityaPanwar, semi-finalist with 5th rank were awarded with a trophy and a cash prize of Rs. 5100/- and Rs.2100/- respectively.


    4. 4. Financial assistance to Govt. Primary school at village Sar-Luni for desks and chairs for the students.
    5. 5. Financial Contribution to Gram PanchayatLuni for JalSwablambanYojna.

    7. 7. Free umbrella distribution to the security guards of Vibrant Academy, Kota.
    8. 8. Constructed a shed and provided water cooler to the Transport office, Ranpur, Kota.
    9. 9. Free distribution of umbrellas to the traffic guards and policemen to provide a relief in summers and rainy season. It was a huge program graced by the officials of trusts and Police and Traffic department, Kota.
    10. 10. Tree plantation and tree guard distribution/installation at village Sar-Luni, Jodhpur.
    11. 11. Food and shelter support to the ‘Pad Yatris’,RamdevraPadyatri at Jodhpur-Jaipur Highway.

    13. 13. Tea and biscuit distribution to the public standing outside the banks during notebandi season, SBBJ bank, IL, Kota.

    16. 16. Monthly distribution of ration/clothes and other items to the poor and needy families.
