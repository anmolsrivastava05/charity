Aims and Objectives

The broad objective of the trust is welfare for needy and people in distress, upliftment of society, betterment of schools, education and awareness, development of youth and students, protection of moral values among youth etc. A comprehensive list of aims and objectives has been thought to pursue under the trust which is as follows:


  • To develop Indian culture, nationalism and world citizenship.
  • To construct and run institutions like schools, hostels, research centre, etc. according to guidelines of education department and to achieve the above mentioned aims.
  • To collaborate with similar organization in their programmes for providing education to needy students.
  • To construct, maintain and manage a dharmshala for comfortable stay of travellers.
  • To accept and receive donations from other organizations/firms/ companies/individual both in foreign currency and in Indian Rupees for providing the financial assistance to the students.
  • To arrange and give aid in organizing lectures, teaching programmes, camps and other activities to educate public and enhance awareness.
  • To help and promote education and learning (technical/non-technical) agricultural, engineering, medical, commercial, scientific or any other object of education including post-graduate studies, in all subjects amongst boys and girls in school and colleges or other institutions approved of by the trustees by means of scholarships or payment of fees or providing books or defraying expenses or otherwise however and to continue or carry on necessary activities to promote the said object.
  • To establish or help in the establishment or support and maintenance by donations or otherwise technical institutes or school and colleges for general education or for medical education or domestic science or other educational or research institutions so that the benefit thereof may be available to students.
  • 9. To establish, maintain, support, help by monetary gifts or otherwise institutions such as:-
    1. Public Libraries
    2. Fine Art Classes or Institutions
    3. Industrial Classes of Institutions
    4. Technology Establishments
    5. Cottage and MSME’s
    6. Old Age homes, etc
  • In giving free/subsidized medicines, injections, tonic food and like to needy people.
  • For the general improvement and promotion of agriculture and plantation.
  • To establish, maintain and support by monetary help or otherwise charitable dispensaries or similar other charitable institution for medical relief including infirmaries, convalescence homes, maternity homes, homes for the blind, deaf and dumb and homes for lepers and the like. Homes for incurables and persons suffering from diseases of long duration.
  • To erect or help in erecting buildings for the poor or otherwise providing housing accommodation to the poor at cheap rents of license fees and to establish and maintain sanatorium in any part of India.
  • To construct new wells tanks and maintain & repair existing wells tanks and other sources of water for the benefit of the public or to give monetary help for the same and to provide sanitation for general public or rural development in villages.
  • To establish and help in establishing institutions for the protections and shelter of animals.
  • To give monetary help for relief from natural calamities like floods, draughts, cyclones, tempests, fire, earthquake or the like or otherwise or the gifts, donations and contribution to institutions or persons doing such relief work.
  • To establish, maintain and support of help by monetary gifts or otherwise public gardens, recreation grounds, public swimming pools and the like.
  • For effecting economic amelioration by imparting technical education setting up modern industries and reducing unemployment.
  • For research in all subject including setting up, maintenance and help of research institutions.
  • For the advancement of any other object of general public utility not involving the carrying on of any activity for profit.
  • To organize conferences, seminars and exhibitions related to medical profession to educate the member about hygienic conditions, traffic rules, pollution control, tourism scenario and ecological condition for India in collaboration with any organization.
  • To communicate with public authorities, State and Central Organizations, Public Association and Societies about the health hazards of the poor people, get them better medical facilities in hospitals and dispensaries.
  • To arrange and give aid for organizing camps in India or abroad for the welfare and upliftment of ‘Divyangs’, disabled and ailing persons and to provide medical facility, medicines, or equipment necessary etc.
  • To do the work of social welfare of its own or through any other organizations dedicated to service to people and animals etc.
  • To run the programme of tree plantation or any other action to neutralize pollution.
  • To provide all necessary facilities for the upliftment of orphan, deaf & dumb and poor boys and give donation or help to any other institutions who are doing the same job.
  • To start welfare centre for the old helpless persons and provide all necessary facilities or to give donations to any other institutions who are doing the same job.
  • To organize competitions for the promotion of art, science, music and culture and to give away prizes in such competitions organized by the trust or others.
  • To propagate the issues of public interest concerning general masses by way of publishing literature, giving public advertisements, organizing meetings etc.
  • To do any other public charitable work, provided that none of the settlor of trustees or their relatives shall be entitled to obtain any benefit from the trust.
